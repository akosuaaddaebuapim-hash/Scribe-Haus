About Scribe Haus

Hi, I’m Akosua. Scribe Haus is a creative studio for books, writing, editing, design, branding, and digital projects. I work with individuals, businesses, and creators across all genres and industries.

From memoirs to fiction, poetry, educational resources, websites, and brand identity, every project is crafted with care and attention to detail.

Books & Journals

At Scribe Haus, books are at the heart of what we do. From uplifting children’s affirmations to personal journals, poetry, memoirs, and fiction, every book is created with the goal of inspiring, guiding, or simply entertaining.

We believe stories and words have the power to shape lives — whether it’s a daily journal for self-reflection, a children’s book filled with positivity, or a full-length novel. Our collection continues to grow, blending creativity with purpose.
